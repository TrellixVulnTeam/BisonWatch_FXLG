⌂ | Stacked Bar Chart

This visualisation shows the courses grouped by a selectable attribute and separates the frequencies by colour according to the faculties. The courses can be grouped according to language, semester hours, type of course, day and faculty.

Select the attribute you are interested in:

Short tips on how to interpret the diagram:

  • When hovering over a column block, the number of events belonging to this block is displayed.
  • The colours of the blocks correspond to the colours of the faculty pages on the Bauhaus University website. Courses that are not assigned to a faculty are coloured grey.
