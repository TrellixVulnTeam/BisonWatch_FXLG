Beschreibung :3 S: The seminar serves as preparation for the Study Project semester in spring/summer 2021. Besides an introduction to the Model Projects Reflective Urban Studies, students will learn to present their personal portfolios for the prospective study project partners at an early stage in the semester. This also consist of individual presentations.
