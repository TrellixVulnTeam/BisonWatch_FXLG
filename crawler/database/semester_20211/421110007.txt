# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: Scientific publications can be regarded as , <br/>, contributions to a complex asynchronous and multi-threaded conversation , <br/>, about specific research questions. Scientists develop and describe their , <br/>, own ideas and relate them to and compare them with previous related , <br/>, ideas and concepts in the field. In this project, you will learn how to , <br/>, apply neural natural language processing technologies and parallel , <br/>, computing to make conceptual relationships explicit in a datastructure , <br/>, called a concept graph. In a concept graph, two concepts are connected , <br/>, by a directed edge if one relates to the other. It can further be , <br/>, developed into a concept prerequisite graph with directed edges if the , <br/>, concepts build upon another. During the project, we would like to create , <br/>, and publish a concept graph as a resource for CORD-19, an open dataset , <br/>, of currently 280K scholarly articles about the coronavirus.
