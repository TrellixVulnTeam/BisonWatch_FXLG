# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: After the explosions in the port of Beirut in August 2020, the student research seminar “Beirut: Stunde Null– Shaping the Reconstruction” brought together knowledge about Beirut and established contacts with local people and organisations as well as Notre Dame University. The results will be published as an online platform and provide the basis for further collaboration.
