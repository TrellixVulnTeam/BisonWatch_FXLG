# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: This seminar combines approaches from different historical and social sciences in order to investigate the global complexity of the built environment. With four groups of students, each guided by one of the researchers from the Leibniz Institute for Research on Society and Space we would like to work on four regional foci, using a range of research methods. Instead of pursuing a fixed schedule we intend to explore various topics in a form of a research laboratory.
