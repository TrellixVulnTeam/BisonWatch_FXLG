# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: <strong>NOTE: This seminar should be taken in tandem with the workshop ”Sneakey Translator”, led by Gabriel Ben Moshe.</strong>, <br><br>There is no doubt about what has brought the concept of translation into the focus of social researchers, cultural theorists, scientists and, not least, artists – the globalization and the rising complexity of the world in which we live today. Centuries old picture of the world as a cluster of sovereign nation states and different cultures; the concept of knowledge meticulously divided into various disciplines and distributed among separate communities of peers; the idea of art enclosed into its own autonomous sphere, are crumbling before our eyes. The course will explore what has made translation – usually understood as an auxiliary form of a proper linguistic practice – a conceptual remedy for the growing chaos in our minds and lives.</br></br>
