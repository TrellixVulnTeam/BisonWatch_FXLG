# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: The course will focus on the development of a simple game, which will foster the imagination of interaction mechanisms between computational machines and the player. For the development of the game, we will use Max/MSP/Jitter visual programming language. And, if needed, we will use Arduino microcontrollers to sense and/or to trigger the physical world.
