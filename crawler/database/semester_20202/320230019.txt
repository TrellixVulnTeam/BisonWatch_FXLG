# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: There is a curious coincidence of three events that occurred simultaneously thirty years ago: the end of the Cold War, also known as the fall of Communism; the end of history; and the ”turn to memory”, the emergence of the so-called ”age of commemoration.” The course shall explore mutual interference of these three events and search for their cognitive, cultural and artistic synergies.
