# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: This seminar combines approaches from urban and architectural history and theory in order to investigate the complexity of the built environment in the long 20, <sup>th</sup>,  century. Starting in the late colonial period, moving on to the ruptures of decolonisation and changing spatial orders of the Cold War, the seminar will also examine how the legacies of these governance regimes continue to shape cities today. Aiming to challenge the entrenched positionalities of Eurocentric research traditions, the regional focus is shifted to the so-called Global South (as contrasted with Global North).
