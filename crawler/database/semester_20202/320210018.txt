# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: In one of the most well-known quotes, Michel Foucault claims “Where there is power, there is resistance” (1978:95–96). The notion of artistic activism and its relation to power has led to development of new artistic strategies in contemporary art. On the contrary Lila Abu-Lughod states “where there is resistance, there is power” (1990: 42) thus understanding resistance is helpful to recognize power. It seems necessary from the desire of understanding we strengthen resistance studies.
