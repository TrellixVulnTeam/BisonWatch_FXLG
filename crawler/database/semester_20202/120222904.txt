# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: Land is the basis of livelihood. As such, it is closely interwoven with social, economic as well as political and ecological interests, claims, and challenges. Land is a limited, immobile and non-reproducible resource that produces and reproduces social conditions. Although land in capitalist societies foremost functions as an economic asset, it remains inseparable from the various social and cultural meanings attached to it.
