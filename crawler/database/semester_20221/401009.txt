# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: The students obtain deepened knowledge in structural dynamics, structural dynamic analysis, data processing, dynamic test equipment and its handling. They learn to analyse the dynamic behaviour of a structure utilizing both numerical and experimental state-of-the-art methods. Furthermore, the students have to develop strategies and concepts of investigation. The work in small groups enhances the social competence of the students.
