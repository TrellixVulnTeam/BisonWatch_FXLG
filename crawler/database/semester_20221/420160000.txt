# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: This course gives an overview of basic techniques of working with language data. We will introduce basic linguistic notions, issues involved in building and working with language corpora, current standard techniques for preparing text for analysis, and methods of computational processing of a subset of language phenomena.
