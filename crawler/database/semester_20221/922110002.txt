# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: Art and Train is a practice class – in cooperation with Deutsche Bahn (DB) – on theory and methods of researching the aesthetics of train travel and the unexplored potential of disused railway architecture. This class takes place both on university campus and on regional trains. Structured on three blocks – theory, train travel (a few days in May), and exhibition (a few days in June) – the class invites students of all disciplines to learn about methods of transforming own train journeys into works of art and to present it in a public exhibition.
