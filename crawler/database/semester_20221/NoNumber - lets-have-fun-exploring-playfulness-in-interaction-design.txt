# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: Playing is fun, everyone likes it. But what is that makes us enjoying to play? What means , <em>to play</em>, ? What is , <em>playfulness</em>, ? Can we design better products if we base interaction concepts on aspects of playfulness? In this course, we will engage with such questions, critically discuss different perspectives and try to find meaningful answers.
