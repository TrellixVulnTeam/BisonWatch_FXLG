# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: This course is designed as an introduction to working with interfaces between living organisms and computing machines. The weekly course is divided into a theoretical and a practical parts. In the theoretical part we will discuss artworks in relation to bioelectronics. , <br>The practical part will include work with organisms and interfaces that connect organisms to computers.</br>
