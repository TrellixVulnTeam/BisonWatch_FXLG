# Beschreibung
Beschreibung :3 S: Physiological computing applies physiological data (like brain waves, skin conductance changes, pupil dynamics or heart rate variability) to generate user-state representations and enable computer systems to dynamically adapt to changes in cognitive and/or affective processing. By connecting the brain/body to a machine, the boundaries of the nervous system are extended which enables us to communicate with machines directly via processes that underlie our thoughts and emotions.
